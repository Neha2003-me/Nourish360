Global Hunger: The Stark Reality

As of the latest estimates:

  • 768 million people suffer from chronic undernourishment.
  • Nearly 149 million children under 5 are affected by chronic malnutrition.
  • Roughly one-third of all food produced globally, equivalent to about 1.3 billion tons, is lost or wasted.
  • Asia is home to the largest number of undernourished people, with more than half of the world's hungry living in this region.
  • Each year, hunger and malnutrition are linked to the deaths of about 3.1 million children under the age of five.
